By Air

Traveling from Kolkata to Lakshadweep by air involves a combination of flights, as there are no direct flights between these two locations. Here is a general guide to making this journey:

    Flight from Kolkata to Kochi:
  • You need to fly from Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport (CCU) in Kolkata to Cochin International Airport (COK) in Kochi, Kerala. Several airlines operate on this route, including IndiGo, Air India, SpiceJet, and Vistara.
      • Flight from Kochi to Agatti Island:
  • From Kochi, you can take a flight to Agatti Aerodrome (AGX) in Lakshadweep. Air India operates flights between Kochi and Agatti Island. These flights are usually on smaller aircraft due to the limited runway length at Agatti.
      • Transfer from Agatti Island to other islands in Lakshadweep:
  • Depending on your final destination within Lakshadweep, you may need to take a boat or helicopter from Agatti Island to other islands such as Kavaratti, Minicoy, or Bangaram.

    • By Ship

    Traveling from Kolkata to Lakshadweep by ship is not a straightforward journey since there are no direct passenger ships operating between these two locations. However, you can make this journey by combining train or air travel with a ship journey from Kochi to Lakshadweep. Here’s how you can do it:

      Travel from Kolkata to Kochi:
  • By Air: The fastest way is to take a flight from Kolkata to Kochi.
  • By Train: Alternatively, you can take a train from Kolkata to Kochi. The train journey takes around 36-40 hours.
      • Kochi to Lakshadweep by Ship:
  • The Lakshadweep administration operates passenger ships from Kochi to Lakshadweep. These ships typically depart from the Kochi Port. The main ports in Lakshadweep that receive ships are Agatti, Kavaratti, and Minicoy.
  • Booking: You need to book tickets for the ship in advance through the Lakshadweep Tourism Development Corporation (LTDC) or the Shipping Corporation of India. These ships have limited capacity and schedules can vary, so early booking is essential.
      • Arrival at Lakshadweep:
  • Upon arrival at Lakshadweep, you’ll need to transfer to your specific island destination. This might involve additional boat rides or local transportation.
